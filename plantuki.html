What is Plantuki?

Plantuki is a virtual pet... Well, not exactly, it is a carnivorous plant.
You will have to feed, water and clean it. As well as control how much light it gets from the Sun.
During the development I was the 3D artist and animator in charge of the plant and pots.
Also, I did some UI design.
